Privacy Policy - Malayalam Catholic Bible Quiz

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Malayalam Catholic Bible Quiz ("the App") accesses, uses, stores, and shares user data when you use the App.

This policy applies to the Android app published as Malayalam Catholic Bible Quiz on Google Play.

1. Data We Access, Collect, and Store

Data type Purpose Where stored
Quiz activity data (for example, quiz attempts, scores, answer details) To show quiz results and support progress/history features On your device; and in cloud storage when sync is enabled
App settings (for example, timer and notification preferences) To remember your preferences On your device
Anonymous account identifier (Firebase anonymous UID, if configured) To associate synced data with your app session Firebase Authentication / Firestore
Push token (if notifications are enabled and permission granted) To send app notifications Firestore
Basic technical diagnostics (for example, request failures or sync errors) To maintain app reliability and troubleshoot issues Device logs and/or backend logs

2. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not intentionally collect highly sensitive personal data such as:

  • Contacts, call logs, SMS, photos, microphone audio, or precise location
  • Government IDs, financial account details, or payment card data

3. How We Use Data

  • To provide quiz functionality and store your quiz progress
  • To sync data between app sessions (when cloud features are active)
  • To deliver notifications only when you enable them
  • To improve stability, performance, and security of the App

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by service providers used to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Storage) for account/session handling and optional cloud sync.
  • Expo notification services for push token handling and notification delivery.

Third-party processing is subject to those providers' terms and privacy policies.

5. Permissions

The App may request notification permission (for daily reminders and push notifications). You can disable notifications in app settings or device settings at any time.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously work to protect user data.

7. Data Retention

  • Local device data remains until you clear app data or uninstall the App.
  • Cloud-synced records are retained while needed to provide app functionality.
  • We may retain limited records as required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute resolution.

8. Data Deletion Requests

You can request deletion of cloud-stored data by contacting us at the email below. Include details that help us locate your records (for example, app/account context and device details).

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age defined by applicable local law. If you believe a child submitted personal data, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

10. International Processing

Depending on your location and the service providers used, data may be processed in countries other than your own.
